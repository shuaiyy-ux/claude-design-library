# Minimal / Swiss

Descended from the International Typographic Style (Müller-Brockmann, Hofmann). The opposite of maximalism.

## Key rules
- **Grid**: 12-column, strictly followed.
- **Type**: Helvetica, Neue Haas Grotesk, or Inter. One family, many weights.
- **Color**: black + white + one accent (red is classic). That's it.
- **Hierarchy**: type size and weight only — no boxes, no borders.
- **Space**: more than feels comfortable. Then more.
- **Alignment**: left or flush — never justified.
